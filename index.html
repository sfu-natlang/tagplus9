Welcome

Welcome to the home page for TAG+9 2008, the 9th International Workshop on Tree Adjoining Grammars and Related Formalisms. TAG+9 2008 will be held on June 7-8 at the University of Tübingen, Germany.

Please refer to the Guidelines for Formatting Papers for the Proceedings and submit your paper for the TAG+9 proceedings by April 28, 2008. You can submit your PDF file by email to tagplus@sfb441.uni-tuebingen.de or upload the PDF file to EasyChair (instructions will be emailed to you).

Important Dates

  • Deadline for submission: Mar 6, 2008 (deadline extended)
  • Notification to authors of decision: Apr 10, 2008
  • Camera ready: Apr 28, 2008
  • Tutorial date: Jun 6, 2008
  • Workshop date: Jun 7-8, 2008
