Tutorial Program at TAG+9

The local organizer, Laura Kallmeyer, has arranged for a tutorial program on Jun 6th, the day preceding the start of TAG+9. Tutorials will be free for those who are registered for TAG+9.

The tutorial program (Jun 6, 2008):

  • 9.15am - 10.45am: Syntax and LTAG (Bob Frank)
  • 11.15am - 12.45pm: TAG Parsing (Eric de la Clergerie)
  • 2.15pm - 3.45pm: Metagrammars, Examples: #1, #2, #3, #4, #5. (Denys Duchier)
  • 4.15pm - 5.45pm: LTAG Semantics (Maribel Romero)
